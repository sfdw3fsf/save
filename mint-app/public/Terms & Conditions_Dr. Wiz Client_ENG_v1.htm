Dr. Wiz Client Terms of Service

 

Corporate or Institutional Members (hereinafter referred to as the "Client") agree to the following terms regarding the use of the Dr. Wiz services, offered by Mint Venture Partners (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") for advisory and consulting purposes.

 

1. Purpose of the Terms

These terms outline the rights, responsibilities, and other necessary provisions between parties regarding the Dr. Wiz service, which is provided when the Client requests expert advice or consulting and the Company selects a suitable expert to fulfill that request.

 

2. Definitions

1. The Dr. Wiz service provides corporate or institutional clients with access to experts in the bio-healthcare sector for advisory and consulting services.

2. In the Dr. Wiz program, the terms “Project, ,” “Advisory, ,” and “Consulting ,” are defined as follows:

- Project: A process in which a Dr. Wiz Expert, a field-specific expert, provides professional opinions or delivers certain results based on a specific topic requested by the Client. It includes both advisory and consulting services.

- Advisory: Refers to one-time or recurring projects.

- Consulting: Refers to projects that focus on achieving specific deliverables on a particular topic over a specified period.

3. Expert refers to individuals who provide comprehensive advisory services for the planning and operation of the Dr. Wiz program, including members of the "Dr. Wiz Board" and "Dr. Wiz Experts," who deliver direct consulting and advisory services. "Dr. Wiz Board" consists of professors with previous experience as hospital directors or medical college deans and provides advice on planning and implementing the Dr. Wiz program while participating directly in projects. "Dr. Wiz Experts" include university hospital professors, clinical physicians from other medical institutions, and professionals in the bio-healthcare industry specializing in business development, regulatory affairs, intellectual property, finance/accounting, and marketing.

4. Dr. Wiz Experts participating in advisory or consulting projects are selected based on the Company’s recommendation and finalized with the Client’s consent.

 

3. Dr. Wiz Service Agreement and Provision of Service

1. Clients must submit requests for advisory services through the "Advisory Consulting Request Form" available on the Mint MD website (www.mintmd.bio). These Terms are provided in the "Advisory Consulting Request Form," and Clients must agree to them to proceed.

2. During the expert selection process specified in Article 3, the Client and Company will enter into a separate “Dr. Wiz Service Agreement” based on the agreed-upon scope and type of service. If there are discrepancies between the individual agreement and these Terms, the individual agreement shall prevail.

3. After the Dr. Wiz Service Agreement is signed, the Company may request any necessary documents for advisory or consulting purposes from the Client. The Client is responsible for providing the requested documents within seven days unless otherwise specified; any delays in document provision will be the Client's responsibility.

4. For quality assurance, client service improvements, and regulatory compliance, the Company may record or store audio or video versions of advisory or consulting meetings between Clients and experts, with prior consent from the Client.

5. Follow-up Procedures

- If the advisory is on a postpaid hourly rate basis (Article 6.2), the Client must submit documentation to verify accurate time calculation within three days of project completion.

- The Client may provide comprehensive evaluations of Dr. Wiz services and Dr. Wiz Experts, including time documentation for each advisory or consulting request.

- Clients may also evaluate meeting participants related to advisory or consulting requests.

 

4. Client Responsibilities

1. Clients shall not request advice or consulting on behalf of any institution or company that an expert may be affiliated with as an employee, consultant, or agent, or for any competitor with conflicting interests.

2. Clients may not request discussions or disclosures of information subject to the expert's confidentiality obligations.

3. If a conflict of interest arises during the advisory or consulting process, the Client must immediately halt the engagement and notify the Company.

4. Without prior written Company consent, Clients may not attempt direct consultations with, or offer employment contracts or agreements to, any experts introduced by the Company within one year of the latest project date. Specific guidelines are as follows:

- Clients cannot engage in direct advisory or consulting services, or enter into similar service contracts, with the expert.

- Clients may not provide payments or benefits directly to the expert.

- Clients may not request follow-up advisory or consulting services without involving the Company.

- Clients may not propose employment or appointments (including external directorships or scientific advisory roles) to the expert without going through the Company.

- Violating these guidelines and directly contacting experts may result in the Company invoicing the Client for an equivalent consulting fee. Additionally, if the Client hires an introduced expert as an employee without prior Company approval, the Client will pay 30% of the expert's first-year compensation (salary and incentives included) to the Company.

5. Clients must not disclose any confidential information acquired through Dr. Wiz services to external parties or unrelated internal personnel, including but not limited to:

- Information protected under Article 10’s confidentiality obligations.

- Personal details of the expert providing advisory or consulting services.

6. Clients must not persuade experts involved in advisory or consulting to prescribe, recommend, sell, or order any drugs, devices, or services.

 

5. Cancellation of Advisory or Consulting Services

Clients have the right to cancel advisory or consulting services within the first 15 minutes at no cost as per Article 4.3. Beyond this timeframe, services cannot be canceled.

 

6. Dr. Wiz Service Fees

1. Fees for Dr. Wiz advisory or consulting projects are determined based on the fee schedule attached to each separate agreement. Clients can choose either an Hourly Plan, billed per hour, or a Credit Plan, where a prepaid credit amount is used per consulting hour.

2. For Hourly Plans, the Client must submit documentation verifying the time calculation within three days after project completion. The Company will tally the total advisory hours at the end of each month and invoice the Client, who will pay the fee within ten business days of the next month.

3. For Credit Plans, the Client must remit the fee within ten days of the Dr. Wiz service agreement completion.

4. Fees for consulting projects shall follow the separate individual service agreement established between the Client and Company.

5. If an advisory or consulting service is canceled according to Article 5, the Client is not required to pay the service fee.

6. For Credit Plan refunds, the Company may deduct a 10% fee from the total service amount prior to applying the discount rate on the service fee schedule.

 

7. Intellectual Property Rights

1. All intellectual property rights for content created independently of the Project (referred to as “Expert Content”) belong to the expert.

2. Intellectual property rights for any content created during the Project (referred to as “Project Content”) belong to the Client who requested the Project.

3. The Company may use the “Project Content” as needed to provide Dr. Wiz services to the Client, to which the Client agrees.

4. The Company may use the Client's company name and logo learned through Dr. Wiz services on the Company’s website and marketing materials, with prior Client notification and consent.
