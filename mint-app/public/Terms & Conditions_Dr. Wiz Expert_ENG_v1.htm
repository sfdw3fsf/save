2. Definitions

  1. Dr. Wiz is a service under the Mint MD business operated by Mint Venture Partners, where experts in the bio-healthcare field provide advisory and consulting services to clients.
  2. In the Dr. Wiz program, "Project," "Advisory," and "Consulting" are defined as follows:
    • "Project" refers to the process and outcome where a Dr. Wiz Expert provides professional opinions or generates certain deliverables in response to a specific topic requested by the client, encompassing both advisory and consulting services.
    • "Advisory" refers to one-time or repetitive projects.
    • "Consulting" refers to projects that aim for specific deliverables by providing advisory services over a set period of time.
    • "Expert" includes both "Dr. Wiz Board," which provides comprehensive advisory for planning and managing the Dr. Wiz business, and "Dr. Wiz Expert," who directly provides expert advisory services. The "Dr. Wiz Board" consists of professors, including former university hospital directors and medical school deans, who advise on the planning and execution of the Dr. Wiz business and also participate in direct advisory and consulting projects. The "Dr. Wiz Expert" includes clinical doctors from university hospitals, professionals in business development in the bio-healthcare industry, regulatory experts, intellectual property experts, financial/accounting experts, and marketing experts from medical institutions or corporations.

3. Dr. Wiz Service Process

  1. Clients can request advisory or consulting services via the website or the Dr. Wiz online platform.
  2. The Company connects clients with experts based on the advisory and consulting requests, allowing the expert to carry out the project.
  3. The expert reviews and accepts/rejects the client's project request through the online platform, and once the client agrees, the project proceeds. The expert implements the Dr. Wiz service as a non-permanent advisory board member after signing a separate contract with the Company.
  4. The advisory service format for a project is primarily conducted One-on-One, where one expert provides advisory services to one client, with online meetings being the default. However, depending on the agreement between the client and expert, the format may include Focus Group advisory (where multiple experts provide advisory), User Survey for expert opinion gathering, or Written Advisory in written form.
  5. For One-on-One, all processes, such as reviewing, accepting/rejecting advisory requests, choosing advisory schedules, and providing advisory, are to be carried out within the online platform.
  6. Depending on the client's request and the expert's consent, a project may also proceed as a consulting project, which provides advisory services over a set period to produce specific deliverables. For consulting projects, the scope, consulting fees, service format, consulting period, and deliverables are separately negotiated between the Company, expert, and client, and formalized in a separate contract.
  7. The Company facilitates smooth progress for the project by connecting the client and expert, ensuring the project proceeds at a mutually agreed-upon time.
  8. Compensation for the expert's advisory or consulting services is calculated based on an hourly rate for One-on-One and Focus Group formats or a set fee for User Survey and Written Advisory formats. Compensation is calculated only for time directly related to project execution, such as advisory provision, and does not include preparation or waiting time unless otherwise agreed with the client.
  9. The Company may record or document online (video) meetings for quality control and regulatory compliance purposes, and the expert agrees to this. If recorded, the Company will notify the expert in advance.
  10. The Company and client may review the recorded content as mentioned in clause 7 for purposes such as reviewing conflicts of interest or service quality.
